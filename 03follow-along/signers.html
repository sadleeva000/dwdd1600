Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Here are some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence:

Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration of Independence and later the 3rd President of the United States.
John Adams
A leading advocate for independence, later became the 2nd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
A renowned inventor, diplomat, and statesman. He played a major role in securing French support during the Revolutionary War.
John Hancock
President of the Second Continental Congress; known for his large, bold signature on the Declaration.
Samuel Adams
A key organizer of the Boston Tea Party and an influential revolutionary leader.
Roger Sherman
Only person to sign all four great state papers of the U.S.: the Continental Association, Declaration of Independence, Articles of Confederation, and the Constitution.
Robert Morris
Major financier of the American Revolution and later helped create the Bank of North America.

These men were instrumental in the founding of the United States and the creation of its government. They were not only signers, but also played important roles in shaping the foundation of the United States.
