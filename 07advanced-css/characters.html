Characters by Arc

Original Sin

Behemo Barisol (ビヒモ＝バリーゾール)
Vocaloid portrayal: Len Kagamine (鏡音レン)
The closest to a protagonist in the series besides maybe his older twin sister and one of the few characters who recurs frequently throughout the entire series. He was related to the wealthy developer of a virtual world, and created his own avatar, which would later become Levia. Since he was young, he was feminine and often faced challenges related to his identity. Because of the technological advancements of the virtual world, he was able to explore and express his identity in ways that were not possible in the real world. This also allowed him to later join the team that would create the Third Period, ascending him to basically godhood, first as one of the Twin Dragon Gods and then as the Master of the Heavenly Yard.
Levia Barisol (レビア＝バリーゾール)
Vocaloid portrayal: Rin Kagamine (鏡音リン)
The other half of the Barisol siblings, often seen as the more morally ambiguous counterpart to Behemo. She was created as Behemo's ideal self and was smart. So smart, she attended college at just six years old. After the spread of the disease HERS (Hereditary Evil Raiser Syndrome), she became fascinated with the idea of evil and became a scientist to study the very question of why evil exists and how humans are capable of doing horrific things. Levia ended up contracting the disease herself and sought to find a cure. With the help of her colleague, Seth, she found the First Period and the truth about her own existence. Believing it would cure her, she attempted to kill Behemo, but soon realized that would only make it worse and instead offered to take Behemo to her world. She is another one of the team responsible for the creation of the Third Period and consequently, another deity. She is the other half of the pair of Twin Dragon Gods and becomes Master of the Hellish Yard. She also is a frequently recurring character throughout the series and like her brother, reincarnates into several other characters.
Seth Twilight (セト＝トワイライト)
Vocaloid portrayal: Kiyoteru Hiyama (氷山キヨテル)
One of the main antagonists of the series. He is an artificial species of sentient masks who never felt understood. Eventually, he contracted HERS and worked alongside Levia. When she came to him for help, he suggested that she kill her "other half", meaning Behemo. However, he did this so he could take her place aboard an ark with 72 other scientists sent to leave this world and find a new one to start anew. Levia would come back with Behemo and Seth would stow away on the ship anyway. Through the rest of the series, he frequently shows up and creates chaos wherever he goes. His main goal is to spread HERS and with it, violence and destruction. Though, he was one of the survivors of the ark's crash in the Third Period, which he caused, he never became a god, but would eventually become the Demon of Wrath.
Held Yggdra (エルド＝ユグドラ)
A prominent biologist, professor, and mentor to Levia during the Second Period. After his child became a HER, he became one of the leading researchers on HERS and its effects on the human psyche. He was also one of the 72 scientists sent aboard the ark. He was another of the survivors of the crash and became a tree spirit and was known as an evil god. He was a friend and mentor to Elluka Clockworker and sent her on the mission to retrieve the seven deadly sins.
Sickle (シックル)
A survivor of the crash and one of the four gods of the Levin religion along with Levia, Behemo, and Held. He was originally TALOS, the companion robot for the Climb One ark. He is the sun god and took the form of a bat, though his true form is a black box. His sole purpose is to observe humanity, curious what they would do without interference.
Maria Moonlit (マリア＝ムーンリット)
Vocaloid portrayal: Nekomura Iroha (猫村いろは)
Grew up in an orphanage in the Third Period. When she was 14, she was struck by lightning and had a vision of the Twin Dragon Gods, who told her they were her true parents and that they would world would end unless they were given proper vessels to reincarnate into. Because of this, she became the queen and prophet of Levantia. Her name was changed to Alice Merry-Go-Round and she gave birth to twins. It was allegedly a virgin conception, but no one believed her. To prevent a scandal, the children were ordered to be killed. The orders were carried out by Seth, but not before he experimented on the twins. Her prophecy started Project Ma until it's first failure with Eve. Then, her people rose against her, calling her a false prophet. She even began to doubt her visions herself. From then on, she was a puppet queen controlled by others.
Eve Zvezda (エヴァ＝ズヴェズダ)
Vocaloid portrayal: Miku Hatsune (初音ミク)
Daughter of Maria Moonlit and wife to Adam Moonlit. After her and her brother were left for dead, she was rescued by a village chief and became a powerful witch. She fell in love with Adam and became the first candidate for Project Ma. Unfortunately, the twins Cain and Abel which she was to give birth to were stillborn. and the project was a failure. After that, she and Adam got married in Held's forest and lived in a cottage in the woods where they raised their adopted children, Hansel and Gretel. She was an inheritor of Levia, meaning she could hypnotize people and make them see illusions. She could also summon lightning. She is another frequently recurring character throughout the series and reincarnates into several other characters. She is known as the Original Sin as her death causes the release of the seven deadly sins. She is also associated with the Clockwork Doll and is the Demon of Sloth.
Adam Moonlit (アダム＝ムーンリット)
Vocaloid portrayal: KAITO (カイト)
Son of Maria Moonlit and husband to Eve Zvezda. He was a prominent scientist in Levantia and was in charge of Project Ma. After he and his sister's attempted murder, he was rescued by a forest spirit and then raised in an orphanage. Eventually a man, who was actually Seth in disguise, took him in and eventually told him the truth about his mother. He decided to get revenge on the government and those who tried to kill him and control and keep him from his mother. The best way to do this would be to get involved in Project Ma as the mother of the vessels of the twin dragon gods would become queen, which would make the father the king. So, he went searching for a potential candidate, which he found in Eve after she rescued him from a bandit attack. He then drugged Eve to make her fall in love with him and eventually she was selected to become the Mem Alpha. Unfortunately, the drugs in her system caused the stillbirth of the twins and it was only then, after he had failed, that he realized he genuinely loved Eve. The two of them got married in Held's forest and adopted Hansel and Gretel. He is another frequently recurring character throughout the series and reincarnates into several other characters.
Meta Salmhofer (メータ＝ザルムホーファー)
Vocaloid portrayal: MEIKO (メイコ)
A powerful witch and second candidate for Project Ma. She is a member of the criminal organization Apocalypse and wife to its leader, Pale Noel. Eventually, she was captured and sentenced to execution, but Seth saved her in exchange for her cooperation in his experiments as part of Project Ma. She was to be the mother of the vessels of the Twin Dragon Gods, and succesfully gave birth. However, soon after, she saw them in life support tubes and remembered her own past. She was one of Seth's "ghoul children," an experiment artificiallly creating and cloning people. Pale Noel was another one of these ghoul children. Not wanting her kids to suffer the same fate, she ran away with them, eventually killed by a delusional Eve who saw her as a bear coming after her for two apples she had picked. Thos two apples were, in reality, Hansel and Gretel.
Hansel (ハンゼル)
Vocaloid portrayal: Len Kagamine (鏡音レン)
Adopted son of Eve and Adam Moonlit. He and his sister were found in the forest and raised by Adam and Eve. They were actually the children of Meta Salmhofer, who Eve killed in a delusion. One day, Adam and Eve try to abandon them in the forest. But, this being Hansel, he left a trail of breadcrumbs. He knew she wasn't his real mother, and him and his sister returned home and killed "the evil witch" and her "henchman". This released the seven deadly sins and from then on, Hansel and Gretel are always there. Twins representing the balance of the universe. Hansel representing destruction, Gretel representing creation. He is another frequently recurring character throughout the series and reincarnates into several other characters.
Gretel (グレーテル)
Vocaloid portrayal: Rin Kagamine (鏡音リン)
Adopted daughter of Eve and Adam Moonlit. She and her brother were found in the forest and raised by Adam and Eve. They were actually the children of Meta Salmhofer, who Eve killed in a delusion. One day, Adam and Eve try to abandon them in the forest. But, her brother left behind a trail of breadcrumbs. They knew Eve wasn't their real mother, and Hansel and Gretel returned home and killed "the evil witch" and her "henchman". This released the seven deadly sins and from then on, Hansel and Gretel are always there. Twins representing the balance of the universe. Hansel representing destruction, Gretel representing creation. She is another frequently recurring character throughout the series and reincarnates into several other characters.
Milky Eights (ミルキー＝エイツ)
Vocaloid portrayal: SF-A2 Miki (SF-A2 ミキ)
A prostitute involved with Pale Noel. She was also one of the candidates for Project Ma along with Elluka Clockworker, Irina Clockworker, and Ly Li until she was killed along with the others by Irina.
Ly Li (リ＝リー)
Vocaloid portrayal: Lily (リリィ)
An aristocrat and candidate for Project Ma along with Milky Eights, Elluka Clockworker, and Irina Clockworker. She was killed along with the others by Irina.
Kiril Clockworker (キリル＝クロックワーカー)
Vocaloid portrayal: Kiyoteru Hiyama (氷山キヨテル)
Music-box maker and brother of Irina and fiance to Elluka. He was in charge of the final attempt at Project Ma. After the death of his fianceeand learning his sister caused it, he became sick with grief and consumed by revenge. When he saw a vision of Elluka, which was actually Levia, saying she could be brought back, he followed her instructions and destroyed Levantia. However, he survived and works a lot behind the scenes of the story, pulling the strings and setting up the events to come thanks to his music boxes and his work with Sickle. He is another of the ghoul children created by Seth.
Elluka Clockworker (エルカ＝クロックワーカー)
Vocaloid portrayal: Luka Megurine (巡音ルカ)
Fiancee to Kiril Clockworker, sister to Irina Clockworker, and powerful sorceress. She was a candidate for Project Ma along with Milky Eights, Ly Li, and Irina Clockworker until she was killed along with the others by Irina. When Levia tricked Kiril into destroying Levantia, she was telling the truth. It did bring Elluka back, in a way. But a piece of Levia was in her, too. She lost her memory and became basically immortal. She also worked with the tree spirit Held Yggdra to find the seven deadly sins. She is one of the few characters who recurs frequently throughout the entire series.
Irina Clockworker (イリーナ＝クロックワーカー)
Vocaloid portrayal: Nekomura Iroha (猫村いろは)
Sister to Elluka Clockworker and Kiril Clockworker. She was a candidate for Project Ma along with Milky Eights, Ly Li, and Elluka. She was in love with Kiril, but he was engaged to her sister. She was a HER and all Seth needed to do was suggest that if she killed the others, then she would be queen. So, she did, making them all seem like tragic accidents. She then became queen for a short while before Kiril destroyed levantia. She was gravely injured, but then Seth came and gave her a body to posess. Specifically, the body of a plush cat. It was a cruel joke on his part, but Irina found a new purpose. After learning Elluka was alive, she became obsessed with revenge and opposed her every step of the way, collecting the seven deadly sins before Elluka could. She is another of the ghoul children created by Seth and is another frequently recurring character throughout the series.

Seven Deadly Sins

Lust

Ilotte Venomania (イロッテ＝ヴェノマニア)
Father of Cherubim and Sateriasis Venomania. He was a duke of Beelzenia and generally regarded as a kind and just ruler, but he hated his eldest son, Cherubim, because of his facial deformity. Even more so when his wife committed suicide because of it. He locked his son in the dungeon
Nylpho Venomania (ニルフォ＝ヴェノマニア)
Wife of Ilotte Venomania and mother to Cherubim. After giving birth to Cherubim, she was horrified by his facial deformity and fell into a deep depression. She eventually committed suicide, which further fueled Ilotte's hatred for Cherubim.
Cherubim Venomania (ケルビム＝ヴェノマニア)
Vocaloid portrayal: Kamui Gakupo (神威がくぽ)
Brother of Sateriasis Venomania and son of Ilotte Venomania. He was a duke of Beelzenia and the vessel of lust. Unfortunately, was born with a facial deformity. Because of this, his parents kept him locked away in their mansion until his brother found him. He began sneaking out with his brother regularly and eventually met Gumina Glassred. The three ofthem became friends and played together and Cherubim fell in love with Gumina. By the time he confessed his love, it was too late. She was already engaged to Sateriasis. Overcome with jealousy, he killed his entire family and staff. At the realization of what he had done, he attempted to take his own life. But then, he was approached by a woman in a dark cloak called I.R., who offered a katana to him and told him to use it instead. This would make a contract with the Demon of Lust, and he would become its vessel. He accepted and gained certain powers. Among those, he could alter his appearance. So, he assumed the identity of Sateriasis. These powers came at a cost, however. He would be overcome with lustful desires and had to act on them or else there would be consequences. He formed a harem and slept with basically every woman in the kingdom. This came to bite him in the end, however, when a man by the name of Karchess Krim went looking for his wife and found her in Cherubim's harem and stabbed him in revenge. The illusions Cherubim used began to fade and one by one his lovers abandoned him, the last being Gumina who was the only one to look back as he lay dying and alone on the floor.
Sateriasis Venomania (サテリアシス＝ヴェノマニア)
Vocaloid portrayal: Kamui Gakupo (神威がくぽ)
Half-brother of Cherubim Venomania and son of Ilotte Venomania. He was a duke of Beelzenia and the favored son of his parents. When he found out about Cherubim's existence, he began sneaking him out to the outside world. He was friends with Gumina Glassred, who he would eventually be engaged to, but it was an arranged marriage and he suspected she loved Cherubim instead. He was killed in Cherubim's jealous rage and Cherubim assumed his identity.
Gumina Glassred (グミナ＝グラスレッド)
Vocaloid portrayal: GUMI (グミ)
Childhood friend of Cherubim and Sateriasis Venomania. She was the daughter of a noble family and was betrothed to Sateriasis. She was in love with Cherubim, but didn't realize it until it was too late. She was brainwashed and taken into Cherubim's harem until his death. It was only after that she realized how much she truly loved him. She was a painter, and for some reason could only paint pictures of him. She didn't know why.
Mikulia Greeonio (ミクリア＝グリーオニオ)
Vocaloid portrayal: Miku Hatsune (初音ミク)
Part of Cherubim's harem. She was the only one unaffected by his illusions, but she stayed with him because she believed he was a sort of guardian angel. After his death, she resorted to prostitution to survive. Eventually, she fell in love with a man and married him, changing her name to Mikulia Calgaround living a normal life until her past came back to haunt her. So she had to take care of them. One by one.
Lukana Octo (ルカナ＝オクト)
Vocaloid portrayal: Luka Megurine (巡音ルカ)
A humble tailor and friend of Lilien Turner. She had premonitions and knew the truth about Cherubin, but she was no match for him. She was taken into his harem, too and after, unfortunately, her body would be used by other characters. First Elluka and then Kayo. I.R. wanted her body, but Lukana instead took Elluka's offer to become her body instead, depriving I.R. of a vessel.
Lilien Turner (リリエン＝ターナー)
Vocaloid portrayal: Lily (リリィ)
A humble baker and friend of Lukana Octo. She was also taken into Cherubim's harem.
Maylis Beelzenia (メイリス＝ビルゼニア)
Vocaloid portrayal: MEIKO (メイコ)
A princess of the Beelzenian Empire and part of Cherubim's harem. She would eventually have a child with him, and the result of that bloodline would be the Conchita family.
Yufina Marlon (ユフィーナ＝マーロン)
Vocaloid portrayal: Yufu Sekka (雪歌ユフ)
Queen of the Kingdom of Marlon and part of Cherubim's harem. Her lover was Karchess Krim, who would end up killing Cherubim in revenge. After, she would reunite and run away with Karchess, overthrowing the current powers in Marlon.
Karchess Krim (カーチェス＝クリム)
Vocaloid portrayal: KAITO (カイト)
A count of the Kingdom of Marlon and husband to Yufina Marlon. After his lover had disappeared, he investigated the matter and found her in Cherubim's harem. Disguising himself as a woman, he pretended to be interested in joining the harem and stabbed Cherubim in revenge. After, he and Yufina ran away together and eventually overthrew the current powers in Marlon.
I.R.
An alias of Irina Clockworker and the medium through which she comminicates after her spirit was transferred to a plush cat. She is the one who offers Cherubim the katana to make a contract with the Demon of Lust and sort of becomes his mentor. She later tries to get Lukana Octo to be her vessel, but Lukana instead takes Elluka's offer to become her body instead, depriving I.R. of a vessel.

Gluttony

Banica Conchita (バニカ＝コンチータ)
Vocaloid portrayal: MEIKO (メイコ)
A duchess of Beelzenia and vessel of gluttony. The night of her birth, at a celebratory feast, her family contracted a disease that gave them insatiable hunger. Since she was so young, she obviously did not partake and was the only one to not contract the disease. Still, it affected her when her parents practically forced food down her throat and they began to tease her for her weight gain because of it. She was betrothed to a man named Carlos Marlon, who at first did not like each other but then grew close from a shared desire to travel the world. Obsessed with food since an early age, Banica eventually made a contract with the Demon of Gluttony to taste all the foods in the world without gaining weight or getting sick, leading her to eat the most grotesque foods imaginable. This earned her the nickname, "The Evil Food Eater Conchita". The engagement between her and Carlos would be called off because of her behavior following this, but the two of them would reunite after Carlos disguised himself as a chef in Banica's palace. They would eventually have a child together while Banica's hunger only got worse. Carlos, fearing for Banica, himself, and his child, attempted to poison her and himself. However, she was immune and Carlos died. Eventually, Banica's hunger got to the point she had tasted literally everything and had a strong urge to eat her child. But, she resisted through curiosity and instead realized there was one food she hadn't tried. Herself. Literally consumed by her sin, she herself became the demon of gluttony.
Carlos Marlon (カルロス＝マーロン)
Vocaloid portrayal: KAITO (カイト)
A prince of the Kingdom of Marlon and lover of Banica Conchita. He was betrothed to her as a child, and at first didn't like her until they bonded over a shared desire to travel the world. Due to Banica's behavior after making a contract with the demon of gluttony, the engagement was called off and he lost track of her until he heard whispers of the "Evil Food Eater" and when he found out it was Banica, he had to find her. He also was approached by the mage AB-CIR and tasked with retrieving the red goblet which served as the object vessel for Gluttony. Carlos disguised himself as a chef in her palace by the name of Josef Crim, but would eventually be found out. The lovers would fall in love again and have a child, but Banica's gluttony only consumed her more and Carlos needed to stop her, so he attempted to poison her and himself with a dose of a special medicine he uses to treat a rare genetic disease. However, she was immune and he would die because of it.
