About Me

Hi! My name is Evan Sadler and I've been a fan of Evillious for a few years now, and Vocaloid even longer. It all started when I stumbled upon the song, "円尾坂の仕立屋 (Enbizaka no Shitateya)" or "The Tailor of Enbizaka". I was already very interested in the story from that one video, so when I learned it was only a part of a series of over 80 songs, I had to check it out.
