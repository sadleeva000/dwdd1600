Glossary

Term Definition
E.C. The standard annual measurement of time used in the Evillious universe established by Queen Alice Merry-Go-Round after the birth of Hansel and Gretel. Stands for "Evillious Chronicle".
B.T. The standard annual measurement of time used in the Evillious universe referring to before the birth of Hansel and Gretel. Stands for "Before Twins."
Ma The title given to the mother of the vessels of the Twin Dragon Gods and eventually the name Ma would choose for herself. Stands for "Mem Alpha".
Project Ma The project selecting the mother of the vessels of the Twin Dragon Gods and future queen.
Venomania Event The massacre of the Venomania family by Cherubim Venomania.
Climb One The ark of the Second Period created to salvage humanity.
Clockwork Lullaby A reccuring musical theme in the Evillious Chronicles and a powerful spell in-universe. Often occompanied by the lyrics "Lu-Li-La Lu-Li-La" and associated with Kiril Clockworker's Black Box.
Venom A powerful mind-altering brainwashing drug.
Apocalypse An anti-government criminal organization led by Pale Noel.
Vocaloid A Japanese voice synthesizing software utilizing a library of sounds called a "voicebank" and often represented by an anime-style character.
Utau A Japanese voice synthesizing software similar to Vocaloid, but free and open-source.
Mothy Creator of the Evillous Chronicles. Stands for "Master of the Heavenly Yard". Also known as Akuno-P.
