What is the Evillious Chronicles?

The Evillious Chronicles is a Japanese multimedia series of interconnected Vocaloid songs, manga, light novels, and more created by Japanese musician Akuno-P (also known as MOTHY). The series is known for its complex narrative, rich world-building, and dark themes. It explores the rise and fall of various characters and civilizations within the fictional world of Evillious, often delving into themes of sin, redemption, and the cyclical nature of history.

Who is MOTHY?

MOTHY, also known as Akuno-P, is the creator of the Evillious Chronicles. He is a Japanese musician and producer who gained fame for his Vocaloid songs. MOTHY is known for his intricate storytelling and world-building, which are central to the Evillious Chronicles. His work has garnered a dedicated fanbase both in Japan and internationally.

What is Vocaloid?

Vocaloid is a singing voice synthesizer technology developed by Yamaha. It allows users to create vocal tracks by inputting lyrics and melodies, which are then sung by virtual singers. The Vocaloid software uses voice banks recorded from real singers to produce realistic singing voices. The technology has been widely used in the creation of music, particularly in the Japanese pop and electronic music scenes. Many Vocaloid characters, such as Hatsune Miku, have become cultural icons and are featured in various media, including music videos, concerts, and merchandise.
